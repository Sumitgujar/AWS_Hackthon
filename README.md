# AWS_Hackthon

🇮🇳 Bharat Setu — AI for Bharat Hackathon Project

Bharat Setu is an AI-powered guidance platform built as part of the AI for Bharat Hackathon organized by Hack2Skill in partnership with AWS — a nationwide challenge aimed at encouraging developers to build practical AI solutions for real-world problems in India’s context.

This project focuses on democratizing access to government schemes and public services by simplifying complex information into citizen-friendly guidance using natural language and AI.

🚀 Hackathon: AI for Bharat

AI for Bharat is a structured program that promotes building impactful, AI-powered solutions specifically for Indian problems. It encourages developers and students to move from theory to real, deployable AI applications aimed at driving accessibility, inclusion, and practical benefits for everyday life across Bharat.

This hackathon focuses on:

Real-life challenges and public impact

AI solutions with practical deployment potential

Innovation for community benefit
